Household Survey on Covid-19 Pandemic in India.









Thank you for showing interest in this Household Labour Survey, which is being conducted by a team of researchers from Indian universities, consisting of Dr. Shubhro Sarkar (IGIDR), Dr. Shamim S. Mondal (NMIMS), Dr. Rajendra Prasad Kundu (JNU) and Dr. Prithviraj Guha (Presidency University).

The purpose of this survey is to study how various members of Indian households have changed the way they spend their time as a result of the lockdown, which was put in place to fight the Coronavirus pandemic.

There are no risks in participating in this research beyond those experienced in everyday life. The time needed to complete the survey depends on household size, and takes approximately 6-8 minutes per household member. We encourage respondents to fill in the questionnaire on the desktop version of a browser rather than the mobile one.

The Survey
  • will be repeated once more when the lockdown will not be in effect
  • is not a study done by any Government agency
  • will not collect or share any personal details about you with anyone, and we will only ask for your email address to be able to contact you for a follow-up survey later. (Your information will be assigned a code number that is unique to this study.)
  • will not allow anyone to identify you or any member of your household, as the findings of our study will only be reported in the aggregate.
  • will not be used for any commercial purposes.


The participant or respondent must be 18 years of age or older to consent to participate in this research study.
